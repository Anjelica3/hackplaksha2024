Attempts left: 5

Rules

  • 5 attempts: You have 5 chances to guess the missing word in each quote.
  • Points and penalties
    • Each correct guess earns you 40 points within 15 seconds, decreasing by 8 points with each subsequent attempt for that quote.
    • Using the hint costs you 10 points.
    • Exceeding the 100-second time limit deducts 50 points.

Introduction

Ready to test your wordplay prowess? Dive into a world of enigmatic quotes with "Quotable"! In this captivating game, you'll encounter famous sayings with crucial words cleverly replaced by blanks. Your challenge? Decipher the hidden gems, one letter at a time.

With each correct guess, you'll unveil the true meaning of the quote, unlocking a deeper understanding of its message. But beware, the clock is ticking and wrong answers will chip away at your attempts. Can you decipher the hidden words and claim victory before time runs out?
